Research Current

I work with the Human Computer Interaction Institute on problems related to privacy and security. My current work is with Blase Ur in Lorrie Cranor's lab; we're trying to determine what makes a password strong. In conjunction with what we learn, we're designing a password meter that will nudge users to make stronger passwords without annoying them. My original project proposal, which outlines my work, is available here. The paper describing all we found will be published shortly.
